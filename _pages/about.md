I am currently a third-year PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) and a Research Scientist Intern at [Snap Research](https://research.snap.com/). My research interests encompass 3D/4D Reconstruction/Generation, Animation & Rigging, and Physical Simulation in 3D Vision. I have also worked on Out-of-distribution Segmentation and Domain Adaptation. 

My academic advisor is [Prof. Narendra Ahuja](https://ece.illinois.edu/about/directory/faculty/n-ahuja). I was fortunate to work with [Dr. Varun Jampani](https://varunjampani.github.io/), [Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang](https://faculty.ucmerced.edu/mhyang/), and [Dr. Lu Qi](http://luqi.info/).

I have also worked as a researcher at [Stability AI](https://stability.ai/), [Shanghai AI Lab](https://www.shlab.org.cn/), and [CUHK, SZ](https://www.cuhk.edu.cn/en).

I'm open to discussions and collaborations, so feel free to reach out!

If you're in a similar situation as I was—switching majors, taking a gap year, and feeling anxious—I'd be more than happy to chat with you (WeChat: zh17603602632).
